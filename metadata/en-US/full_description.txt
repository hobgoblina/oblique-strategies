A deck of cards.
Over one hundred worthwhile dilemmas.
Insights into creative processes.
Gnomic challenges, constraints, and questions for you to interpret.

First published by Brian Eno and Peter Schmidt in 1975, Oblique Strategies is a tool for working past creative blocks and reorienting (or disorienting) thought processes.

This app presents you with a single strategy card at a time.
- Swipe to reveal the next card.
- Use the back button to bring previous cards back.
- Create recurring notifications.
- Favorites cards to change how frequently they occur.
